Controls

  • Left arrow - Move left
  • Right arrow - Move right
  • Down arrow - Soft Drop
  • X or Up arrow - Rotate right
  • Ctrl or Space - Hard drop
  • Z - Rotate left
  • P - Pause game
  • Q - Quit game
  • M - Mute sound
  • T - Theme tune
  • K - Faster keys
